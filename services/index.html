What We Offer

Our Electrical Engineering Services

EMA-licensed and fully insured, we deliver the full spectrum of electrical engineering services for industrial, commercial, and institutional clients across Singapore.

⚡

LEW Services

Our EMA-licensed LEW engineers manage all aspects of electrical installation work — from new installations to modifications and disconnections. We act as the Licensed Electrical Worker of Record for your facility, taking full regulatory responsibility for compliance.

  • Appointment as LEW for premises
  • Electrical installation and modification works
  • Periodic inspection and certification
  • EMA notification and submissions
  • LEW turnover and handover services
🔍

Electrical Testing & Inspection

All new and modified electrical installations in Singapore require testing and inspection before they can be energised. Our engineers carry out comprehensive testing to SS 638 and CP 5 standards, providing official test reports accepted by EMA and SP Group.

  • Insulation resistance testing
  • Earth continuity and earth fault loop testing
  • RCD/ELCB trip time testing
  • High voltage (HV) and low voltage (LV) testing
  • Commissioning and final certification
🔧

Shutdown Servicing

Planned electrical maintenance during scheduled facility shutdowns ensures your systems stay reliable, efficient, and safe year-round. We coordinate closely with your facilities team to maximise work done within your shutdown window and minimise disruption.

  • Switchgear and switchboard servicing
  • Transformer inspection and oil sampling
  • Cable termination checks and re-torquing
  • Busbar and connection cleaning
  • Protective relay testing and calibration
📋

EMA Licence Applications

Navigating EMA licensing requirements can be complex and time-sensitive. Our team handles the entire application and renewal process for you — from document preparation to submission — ensuring nothing lapses and your operations remain legal.

  • New LEW licence applications
  • Licence renewal before expiry
  • Category upgrades and amendments
  • Electrical installation licence (EIL) submissions
  • Compliance advisory and gap analysis
💡

Electrical Consultancy

Whether you're planning a new facility, upgrading ageing infrastructure, or optimising energy usage, our consultancy team provides practical, cost-effective engineering advice backed by decades of on-the-ground experience in Singapore.

  • Electrical system design and single-line diagrams
  • Load flow and short-circuit analysis
  • Energy audits and efficiency recommendations
  • Upgrade and replacement planning
  • Regulatory compliance consulting
🏗️

Emergency & Reactive Works

Electrical faults don't follow business hours. Our team is available to respond to emergencies — from tripped main switches to cable faults — to restore power and safety to your facility as quickly as possible.

  • 24/7 emergency response (on arrangement)
  • Fault finding and diagnosis
  • Cable fault location and repair
  • Temporary power arrangements
  • Post-fault safety inspection and certification

Customised In-House Training

We design and deliver electrical safety and technical training programmes built around your specific site, equipment, industry, and team — not a generic off-the-shelf course.

Why Customised Training?

Generic electrical safety courses cover general principles — but every facility is different. A data centre has different electrical risks and procedures than a manufacturing plant. A hospital has different priorities than a commercial office building. A team of experienced LEWs needs different content than a group of newly hired technicians.

Customised in-house training ensures that every minute of training time is directly relevant to your team, your equipment, and your workplace. It also means we can incorporate your actual site drawings, your specific procedures, and your real equipment — making the training immediately applicable rather than theoretical.

Cost-effective at scale: For groups of 10 or more, customised in-house training is typically more cost-effective per head than sending individuals to open courses — and the content will be more relevant to your organisation.

What We Can Cover

We can build a training programme around any combination of the following, tailored to your team's level and needs:

  • Electrical safety awareness and hazard recognition for your specific site
  • Permit-to-Work systems aligned to your existing PTW procedures
  • Electrical testing and inspection techniques for your installation types
  • Shutdown and outage safety procedures for your plant or facility
  • Switchroom and HV equipment safety awareness
  • Emergency response procedures specific to your site layout
  • EMA regulatory compliance — what your team needs to know
  • Contractor management for electrical works

Industries We Serve

  • Data centres and co-location facilities
  • Manufacturing and industrial plants
  • Hospitals, healthcare, and pharmaceutical facilities
  • Commercial buildings and mixed-use developments
  • Logistics and warehousing operations
  • Retail malls and hospitality facilities
  • Government and statutory board facilities

Our Process

1. Needs Assessment
We meet with you to understand your team's roles, existing knowledge, your facility type, and any specific incidents or compliance concerns that prompted the training need.
2. Course Design
We design a course outline, select content modules, and build in any site-specific elements (your drawings, your procedures, your equipment). You review and approve before delivery.
3. Delivery
We deliver the training at your facility or ours, on a schedule that suits your operations. Group size is flexible — we can accommodate anything from a team of 5 to a company-wide rollout.
4. Certification
All participants who complete the training receive a Certificate of Completion. We can also provide a training record summary for your compliance files.

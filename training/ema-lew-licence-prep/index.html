EMA LEW Licence Preparation Course

Two-day intensive preparation for candidates sitting the EMA Licensed Electrical Worker examination — taught by practising LEW engineers with first-hand exam experience.

Course Overview

The EMA Licensed Electrical Worker (LEW) licence is a legal requirement for anyone supervising or carrying out electrical work on electrical installations in Singapore. Obtaining the licence requires passing a written examination set by the Energy Market Authority covering Singapore's electrical regulations, technical standards, and installation requirements.

This two-day preparation course is designed to give candidates the structured revision, technical grounding, and exam strategy they need to pass first time. All sessions are led by our practising LEW engineers — people who have sat and passed the examination themselves and work with Singapore's electrical regulations every day.

Pass rate matters: The EMA LEW examination has a significant failure rate among unprepared candidates. Structured preparation with practising engineers consistently improves pass rates compared to self-study alone.

What You Will Learn

  • Singapore's Electricity Act and subsidiary legislation — key provisions and practical applications
  • EMA licence categories — scope, limitations, and responsibilities of each LEW class
  • Singapore Standard SS 638: Code of Practice for Electrical Installations
  • CP 5: Code of Practice for Earthing
  • Protection systems — fuses, circuit breakers, earth fault protection
  • Cable sizing, voltage drop calculations, and diversity factors
  • Switchboard design and distribution system requirements
  • Exam technique — question interpretation, common mistakes, time management

Who Should Attend

  • Engineers and technicians preparing to sit the EMA LEW examination for the first time
  • Candidates who have sat the examination previously and wish to improve their results
  • Technical staff sponsored by their employer to obtain LEW certification
  • Graduates in electrical engineering entering the Singapore electrical industry

Two-Day Programme

Day 1 — Regulations
Singapore Electricity Act and Regulations in depth. LEW licence categories and scope. EMA's role and enforcement. Notification and approval procedures for electrical installations.
Day 1 — Standards
SS 638 structure and key requirements. CP 5 earthing code. How to read and apply Singapore Standards in examination questions. Common examiner focus areas.
Day 2 — Technical
Protection systems, cable selection, switchgear requirements, voltage drop. Worked calculation examples. Technical drawing interpretation. Past paper question analysis.
Day 2 — Mock Exam
Full timed mock examination under exam conditions. Individual feedback session. Exam strategy and final revision tips from our LEW trainers.

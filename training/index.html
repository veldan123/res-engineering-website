Safety & Compliance

Electrical Safety Training Courses

Practical, accredited electrical safety training for Singapore workplaces — meeting EMA, MOM, and WSH Act requirements for workers, supervisors, and facility managers.

Available Courses

Training Programmes

All courses are conducted by our EMA-licensed engineers and experienced safety trainers. Group bookings and on-site delivery available.

EMA Required

Electrical Safety Awareness Course

Foundational electrical safety training for workers who work near or with electrical systems. Covers hazard identification, safe work practices, and emergency response procedures in compliance with the Electricity Act.

⏱ Half Day (4 hrs) 👥 Up to 20 pax 📜 Certificate Issued
WSH / MOM

Permit-to-Work (PTW) for Electrical Works

Covers the proper implementation of Permit-to-Work systems for electrical isolation, lockout/tagout (LOTO) procedures, and safe re-energisation. Mandatory for supervisors managing electrical maintenance activities.

⏱ Full Day (8 hrs) 👥 Up to 15 pax 📜 Certificate Issued
EMA Required

EMA LEW Licence Preparation Course

Preparatory course for candidates sitting the EMA Licensed Electrical Worker (LEW) examination. Covers Singapore regulations, technical concepts, and exam technique. Conducted by practising LEW engineers.

⏱ 2 Days 👥 Up to 12 pax 📜 Attendance Certificate
Technical

Electrical Testing & Inspection Techniques

Hands-on technical training for engineers and technicians on performing electrical installation testing to SS 638. Includes insulation resistance, earth loop impedance, RCD testing, and report writing.

⏱ Full Day (8 hrs) 👥 Up to 10 pax 📜 Certificate Issued
WSH / MOM

Low Voltage Electrical Safety for Non-Electricians

Designed for facilities staff, supervisors, and managers who need to understand electrical safety without being electricians. Covers what you can and cannot do safely, and when to call a LEW.

⏱ Half Day (4 hrs) 👥 Up to 25 pax 📜 Certificate Issued

About RES Engineering Services

For over 15 years, we have been the electrical engineering partner that Singapore businesses trust — delivering safe, compliant, and reliable solutions every time.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Expertise & Integrity

RES Engineering Services was founded with one goal: to provide Singapore businesses with electrical engineering services they can genuinely rely on.

From our early days handling electrical testing for small commercial premises, we have grown into a full-service electrical engineering firm trusted by manufacturers, property developers, facility managers, and MNCs across Singapore.

Every engineer on our team holds the relevant EMA licences for their scope of work. We don't cut corners — because in electrical engineering, that's what keeps people safe.

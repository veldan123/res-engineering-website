Featured Projects

🏭 Industrial

Annual Shutdown Servicing — Manufacturing Plant, Jurong

Complete switchgear and transformer servicing for a large manufacturing facility during their annual 72-hour planned shutdown. Included HV testing, relay calibration, and cable termination checks across 4 substations.

🏢 Commercial

New Building Electrical Testing — Grade A Office, CBD

Full SS 638 testing and commissioning for a newly completed 30-storey Grade A office tower in the CBD. Covered all LV distribution boards, final circuits, and emergency lighting systems before TOP application.

📋 EMA Licence

Portfolio LEW Management — Property Management Firm

Ongoing LEW appointment and EMA licence management for a property firm managing 12 commercial buildings across Singapore. Includes periodic inspections, renewals, and regulatory submissions.

💻 Data Centre

Electrical Maintenance — Tier III Data Centre, Ayer Rajah

Planned electrical maintenance and testing for a Tier III data centre. Works completed using a carefully sequenced live-maintenance methodology to ensure zero downtime throughout the maintenance window.

🏥 Healthcare

Critical Power Testing — Specialist Medical Clinic

Full electrical testing and ATS (Automatic Transfer Switch) commissioning for a specialist medical clinic. Included generator load testing and UPS system verification to ensure uninterrupted power for critical equipment.

Delivered a customised electrical safety awareness programme for 80+ production workers and supervisors at a large F&B manufacturing facility in Senoko — conducted across 4 sessions over 2 days.
